Guizhou Dong Minority Cookbook

In the special Dong Cookbook section, we present a total of 25 dishes, which are all real dishes documented during our visit, featuring a unique combination of general Chinese family dishes, like sweet and sour tenderloin, and traditional Guizhou Cuisine, like the Crispy Pork Belly. Enjoy exploring the cookbook!

Sweet and Sour Pork Tenderloin

Description: A Guizhou-style sweet and sour pork tenderloin with a crispy outside and tender inside.

Method: Slice pork tenderloin into strips, marinate, coat with flour, fry until golden, and stir-fry with sweet and sour sauce.

Minced Pork with Peas

Description: Crunchy peas and savory minced pork make this a common home dish in Guizhou.

Method: Blanch peas, stir-fry minced pork, then add peas and stir-fry with seasonings.

Ants Climbing a Tree (Vermicelli with Minced Pork)

Description: Vermicelli absorbs the rich flavor of minced pork in this popular Guizhou dish.

Method: Soak vermicelli, then stir-fry with minced pork, bean paste, and other seasonings.

Twice-Cooked Pork

Description: A Guizhou version of twice-cooked pork, with a perfect balance of fat and lean meat.

Method: Boil pork belly, slice, and stir-fry with bean paste, garlic sprouts, and other seasonings.

Minced Pork with Eggplant

Description: Soft eggplant combined with savory minced pork, a simple Guizhou stir-fry dish.

Method: Dice eggplant, stir-fry with minced pork, garlic, and seasonings.

Wood Ear Mushrooms with Lotus Root

Description: Crunchy wood ear mushrooms and sweet lotus root make this a refreshing dish.

Method: Blanch wood ear mushrooms and lotus root, then stir-fry quickly with garlic to keep crisp.

Stir-fried Beef with Green Peppers

Description: Tender beef and crunchy green peppers, a signature Guizhou stir-fry.

Method: Slice beef, marinate, and quickly stir-fry with green peppers and seasonings.

Garlic Stir-fried Greens

Description: Crisp greens stir-fried with garlic, a quick and flavorful Guizhou dish.

Method: Clean the greens and stir-fry quickly with minced garlic and seasonings.

Shredded Potatoes (White)

Description: Crisp and refreshing shredded potatoes, a classic Guizhou home dish.

Method: Julienne potatoes, blanch, and stir-fry with garlic to maintain their white color.

Guizhou Sausages

Description: Spicy and savory sausages, a signature Guizhou snack.

Method: Slice sausages and steam or stir-fry before serving.

Steamed Egg Custard (Soy Sauce)

Description: Silky and delicate steamed egg custard, enhanced with soy sauce.

Method: Whisk eggs with water, steam, and drizzle with soy sauce.

Purple Rice

Description: Soft and sweet purple rice, a staple in Guizhou cuisine.

Method: Rinse purple rice and cook until tender.

Steamed Sweet Pumpkin

Description: Naturally sweet and soft pumpkin, steamed to retain its original flavor.

Method: Cut pumpkin into pieces, steam, and optionally add a little sugar.

Steamed Sticky Rice with Chicken

Description: Soft sticky rice combined with tender chicken, a traditional Guizhou steamed dish.

Method: Mix sticky rice with marinated chicken and steam until fully cooked.

Egg Dumplings

Description: Egg dumplings with a thin skin and fresh filling, a must-have for Guizhou festivals.

Method: Fry egg batter into thin skins, wrap with meat filling, and pan-fry until cooked.

Braised Beef Slices

Description: Flavorful beef slices braised with a rich and savory sauce.

Method: Slice marinated beef, cook, and stir-fry with sauce for added flavor.

Guizhou Crispy Pork Belly

Description: Crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, this pork belly dish offers a unique texture.

Method: Marinate pork belly and roast until the skin is crispy, then slice and serve.

Steamed Chicken with Garlic Soy Sauce

Description: Juicy steamed chicken, paired with a flavorful garlic soy sauce dip.

Method: Steam chicken pieces and serve with a garlic soy sauce dip.

Jujube Cake

Description: Soft and sweet jujube cake, a traditional Guizhou dessert.

Method: Mix jujube paste with flour and steam until fully cooked.

Fried Green Bean Cakes

Description: Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these fried green bean cakes are sweet and delicious.

Method: Cut green bean cakes into pieces and fry until golden.

Tomato Steamed and Fried Egg Cake

Description: Soft egg cake paired with tomatoes for added flavor.

Method: Steam egg cake, then fry with tomatoes until golden.

Grilled Pork Intestines

Description: Crispy and flavorful grilled pork intestines with a distinct smoky flavor.

Method: Clean pork intestines thoroughly and grill until crispy and cooked through.

Grilled Pork

Description: Tender and juicy grilled pork, a must-have at Guizhou night markets.

Method: Slice and marinate pork, then grill until cooked to perfection.
